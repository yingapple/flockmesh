Required Inputs

  • Workspace ID: tenant boundary (all agents/runs stay inside this workspace).
  • Owner ID: actor who starts runs and handles approvals.
  • Template: your first scenario (you can switch later).
  • Workspace Folder: optional, needed only for Claude Code/Codex local file operations.
